Freedom in Eren's case


Does Freedom require sacrifice?

There is a saying that 'You must give up one thing to obtain another thing'. So, Yeah. People sacrifice something to get that freedom.

And in Eren's case, his definition of freedom is clear-cut: he wants to break free from the oppressive walls that have trapped humanity for centuries. His desire for freedom is synonymous with vengeance, but as he uncovers more truths about the world, it shifts. He realizes that the true chains binding humanity are not just the Titans, but the systems of power, oppression, and the cyclical nature of violence and hatred between Paradis Island and the rest of the world.
